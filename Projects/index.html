Life Builder

Life Builder is an interactive web application that simulates various aspects of life, including job progression, skill development, and financial management. Users can choose from a range of jobs, each with its own unique progression system, and track their income, experience points, and other relevant metrics. The application also features a dark mode and debug options for enhanced user customization.
